People & JTBD - June 2026

Jobs to be done

Jobs in the format "When [situation], I want [motivation], so that [outcome]." No feature names - only the person's progress. Every job cites its persona and research source. Hypothesis claims carry a ? badge. Confirmed claims carry no badge.

01

Main job of the product

Main Job
"When I am preparing for a roadmap planning session and I have customer feedback spread across multiple tools I haven't had time to read, I want to feel certain I know what our customers actually need most - with the evidence behind it - so that I can walk into the session with a call I'll stand behind and defend if anyone pushes back."
A - Primary
Note: Stated in the PM's language, not the product's. The product names this "synthesis-to-defensible-decision." The PM names it "I want to not be guessing when I walk into the room."
Source: strategy.md (Segment A primary JTBD). research.md (JTBD Selected jobs). CLAUDE.md (Primary Job). ux-patterns.md B1, B5.

Matrix conclusions

3 jobs for the MVP core

Jobs with importance 3 for Persona A (primary persona) that are not closed by any existing competitor.

MVP Core 01
Confident defensible synthesis for planning (Main Job)
Importance 3 for Persona A. All competitors provide feedback capture and AI classification. None demonstrate, on public pages, a transparent synthesis chain the PM can act on and defend. The gap is at the trust layer, not at the data layer.
MVP Core 02
Know how much to trust a conclusion (J3)
Importance 3 for Persona A. The clearest unclosed gap across all five competitors. No competitor shows sample size and confidence level alongside synthesis conclusions with honest variation (thin evidence vs. strong evidence treated differently). This is the single function most needed to make the main job achievable. Day-one scope locked by D2: item count (n=X) + "low signal" badge.
D2 - Mandatory in MVP. Day-one scope only.
Confidence display is the mechanism that makes H1 true. Without it, the transparency condition fails. Source diversity, time range, and configurable thresholds are v2 - not MVP blockers.
MVP Core 03
Give stakeholders something they can engage with (S1)
Importance 3 for both Persona A and B. Dovetail partially closes this for research teams, not PM roadmap defense. No competitor has a PM-focused shareable evidence brief designed for the "defend your roadmap to leadership" moment. This is both a product function and the primary referral artifact (aarrr.md: the brief drives acquisition, retention, and referral simultaneously).

H1 - Riskiest assumption: status as of June 2026

Updated - live research June 2026
H1 is conditionally confirmed (MEDIUM). When synthesis is transparent and traceable (inline citations, evidence counts, drill-down to raw signal), PMs DO act on AI synthesis without re-synthesizing raw data. Without transparency, H1 fails. Founder decision D2 responds to this: confidence display is mandatory in MVP precisely because transparency is the condition H1 requires. Sources: aipmtools.org, blog.ravi-mehta.com, productboard.com, capterra.com (June 2026). See live-research.md F1, F2, F3, F4. strategy.md Section 5.
