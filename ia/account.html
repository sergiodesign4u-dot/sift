Account / settings

One shell with sections, not four pages: plan and limits (the conversion surface), data and privacy (the PII-scrub default and GDPR controls), and profile. Thin and low-prominence, a platform necessity with no jtbd job. Source: ia/docs/pages/account.md.

SEO and accessibility

  • noindex (private app), robots: noindex, follow, no schema.
  • The usage bar is not color-only (a number and a label, "480 of 500 items this month").
  • Toggles have labels and states; the destructive delete-data action has a confirm; data export and delete are keyboard-reachable (a compliance and trust expectation).
  • Sections mirror one shell, not N pages; targets at least 44px.
