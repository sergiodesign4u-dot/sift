Information Architecture - Basic Layer - July 2026

Concept Map

The concept structure for Sift, derived from jobs, not from a competitor menu: screens grouped by the person's intent, the navigation that moves between them, and the matrix that proves every screen closes a job and every job reaches a screen.

01

Schema by intent

This is the concept: screens grouped by intent, with the job each one closes. States (empty / error / loading) live in the flows; page contents live in the detail layer. Neither belongs here.

Four intent clusters carry every user job; the Platform cluster (Auth, Settings) closes no jtbd job and is kept only as a platform and compliance necessity. The lines show the primary path between clusters, not the flow: the full routes, with states and dead-ends, are on the Flows page.

02

Navigation model

Global navigation is the primary persona's shortest path to the main job, not a menu copied from anyone. Three entries, one per top-level cluster, plus a low-prominence account corner.

SynthesisMain, J2, J3
The default landing and home (to B1). Why the PM opens Sift: the ranked picture of what customers need most. Synthesis-first, per behavioral pattern B1.
SourcesJ2
Manage and add signal inputs (to A1). Setup-heavy during activation, occasional afterward.
BriefsS1, J1, J4
The shareable artifacts (to the brief list and D1). Package the call, defend it, align the team.
Account / SettingsPlatform
In a corner, not a primary tab. Plan and limits, the PII-scrub default, sign out. No user job (ORPHAN), kept for the platform.

Depth to the main job

Main-job view: tap 0 (home) Trace to raw evidence: 2 taps Capture the call: 1 tap

The first screen after auth is the Synthesis view, which is itself the main-job surface, so the ranked, trustable picture is at tap 0. The deepest necessary path (B1 to B2 to C1, the raw source) is 2 taps, within the 3-tap budget. No restructure needed.

Global, contextual, deep

Global

The three entries (Synthesis, Sources, Briefs) and the account corner. Always visible.

Contextual

Theme detail, Evidence source (the drill-down chain), Connect / import, and Build brief. Appear inside a flow.

Deep

Settings / Workspace, Auth, source disconnect, brief manage. Rare. The public Shared brief sits outside the app nav (no login).
