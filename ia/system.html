03

SEO, law, accessibility

  • 8.0 returns HTTP 404 + noindex; 8.2 returns HTTP 503 + Retry-After; 8.1 is transient and not indexed. None is a soft-404.
  • Cookie law (grounded in gdpr.eu): no non-essential cookies before consent; Reject all is as easy as Accept all; analytics and marketing are opt-in, off by default; strictly necessary is always on and explained; a link to the policy; consent is stored and re-openable from the footer.
  • The banner is keyboard operable with equal-prominence actions; the toast uses aria-live; every action is at least 44px.
