SEO and accessibility

  • noindex (transactional), robots: noindex, nofollow, no schema. Auth is never indexed.
  • Labeled inputs, a visible focus ring, errors tied to fields with aria-describedby, keyboard-reachable actions.
  • Reset never reveals whether an email exists (a neutral sent confirmation), an account-safety practice.
  • Errors (invalid email, wrong password, expired link, SSO failure) are inline and recoverable. Targets at least 44px.
