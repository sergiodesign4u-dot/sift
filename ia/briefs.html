Information Architecture - Detail Layer - July 2026
Nodes 6.0 to 6.5 cluster group: pages noindex

Decide and defend / Briefs

Turn the trusted picture into a call, and package it so a stakeholder can read and challenge it without a Sift seat. The list, the build screen, the share-link dialog, and the one public surface: a shared brief that is public to view but never indexed. Source: ia/docs/pages/briefs.md.

Component references

  • Theme card and confidence indicator (owned by 2.0) in selection and in the public view.

04

SEO and accessibility

  • Private nodes: noindex, no schema.
  • 6.4 Shared brief: noindex, nofollow, but a real public URL, so it must be fast, readable, and self-explanatory with no Sift chrome. The token in the URL is the access control; content is PII-scrubbed.
  • No login wall on 6.4 (the whole point). Semantic headings per theme, confidence as text, quotes as real text.
  • An ignored brief is not a dead-end (Flow 4 hands off to defend live). Targets at least 44px.
