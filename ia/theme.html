Theme detail

One theme: its summary, its confidence, and its evidence items with inline citations, each drillable to the raw source. The middle of the trust chain, and the owner of the canonical evidence snippet. Source: ia/docs/pages/theme.md.

01

Blocks and states

#BlockPurpose
1App header (0.1)primary destinations
2BreadcrumbSynthesis > Theme, the drill context
3Theme headerlabel, confidence, summary, Add to brief
4Evidence items (n=X)the canonical snippet, each drills to 5.0

States (nodes)

  • 4.0 default: a strong theme with a full evidence list.
  • 4.1 low-signal variant: the low-signal badge, a thin-evidence caution, and the items still fully drillable. Transparency, not suppression.
  • Loading and error inherit the app patterns; a single unresolvable item is handled by 5.1, not here.

03

Canonical component (owned here)

Evidence snippet (feedback item card)

A verbatim excerpt (truncated with a more affordance), the channel (CSV / Intercom), the date, and an inline citation number tying the snippet to the theme claim. Author references are scrubbed by default. The snippet links to 5.0 Evidence source; if the source cannot resolve, 5.1 handles it.

Referenced by 5.0 (the expanded form), 6.4 (the public quote). Change the snippet here, then walk the references.

04

SEO and accessibility

  • noindex (private app), robots: noindex, follow, no schema.
  • The evidence list is a semantic list; each snippet is a link to 5.0 with an accessible name (source and date).
  • The confidence indicator carries text (n and the badge word), not color alone.
  • The inline citation is a real link labeled ("evidence item 3"), not a bare superscript.
  • The breadcrumb is page-level (Synthesis > Theme), not the global nav. Targets at least 44px.
