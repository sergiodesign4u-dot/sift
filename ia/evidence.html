Nodes 5.0, 5.1 page group: pages noindex

Evidence source

A single raw feedback item in context: the full verbatim text, its channel, date, source, and the theme it supports. The end of the trust chain and the surface the PM pulls up to defend the call live. Source: ia/docs/pages/evidence.md.

01

Blocks and states

#BlockPurpose
1App header (0.1)primary destinations
2BreadcrumbSynthesis > Theme > Evidence, the full chain
3Raw itemfull verbatim, channel, date, scrubbed author, source
4Provenance + linkswhich theme it supports, citation, back to theme

States (nodes)

  • 5.0 default: the full item in context.
  • 5.1 redacted / unavailable: PII-scrubbed beyond display, or the source was removed or cannot resolve. An honest notice, the affected-citation note, and back to theme. The Flow 2 traceability guard: honesty over a false citation.
The chain ends here: theme (4.0) to items (n=X) to this raw source. There is no deeper node, this is the ground truth every claim resolves to (traceable or it does not exist).

03

Component reference

  • This page is the expanded, in-context form of the evidence snippet (canonical, owned by 4.0). It does not re-define the snippet; it renders the full item.
  • Uses Navigation (0.1) and Footer (0.2, app minimal).
04

SEO and accessibility

  • noindex (private app), robots: noindex, follow, no schema. Customer verbatim data must never be indexed.
  • The verbatim text is real, selectable, copyable text (needed to quote it in a meeting).
  • The redaction notice (5.1) is a clear labeled region, never a silent blank.
  • Back-to-theme and citation links have accessible names; targets at least 44px.
