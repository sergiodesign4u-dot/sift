Q3 roadmap: what customers need most

Built from 187 feedback items across CSV and Intercom, PII scrubbed. Prepared by the product team. Every theme below links back to the customer voices behind it.

Recommended priority

  1. 1 Onboarding is confusing n=42
  2. 2 Slow export on large accounts n=28
  3. 3 Confusing billing and invoices n=19

Themes and evidence

1 · Onboarding is confusing n=42

New users cannot tell what to do first. The setup steps, the blank first-run screen, and the initial import are the most cited friction.

"I signed up and just stared at an empty dashboard. No idea what to do first."
"My team gave up during setup because the first-run screen was blank with no guidance."

2 · Slow export on large accounts n=28

Exports time out or lag for accounts with large datasets, blocking reporting workflows.

"Exporting our account data takes minutes and sometimes just fails."
"The bigger our workspace gets, the slower export becomes."

3 · Confusing billing and invoices n=19

Invoices are hard to read and the plan limits are unclear, driving billing tickets.

"I could not tell what I was being charged for on the invoice."
"The plan limits are not clear until you hit them."
