How Sift works

Three steps from scattered signal to a call you can stand behind. It mirrors the path you take inside the app.

  1. 1

    Connect a source

    Import a CSV export or connect Intercom. Feedback is normalized on the way in, and PII is scrubbed by default.

  2. 2

    See ranked themes with confidence

    Sift clusters the feedback into themes and shows how strong each one is, so you can tell a real pattern from a thin one.

    n=42
  3. 3

    Trace every claim to evidence

    Open any theme to read the exact customer quotes behind it. If a claim cannot be traced, it is marked, not dressed up.

Built to be trusted, not taken on faith

Sift handles sensitive feedback, so the same signal shows up here and in the footer.

  • Transparency

    No black box. Every synthesis conclusion links to the evidence behind it, and confidence is shown honestly. Thin evidence is never dressed up as strong signal.

  • Traceability

    Theme, to evidence items (n=X), to the verbatim quote. Every priority traces to the real user voice behind it, so you can defend the call under challenge.

  • PII scrubbed by default

    Names and email addresses are stripped from feedback on import. GDPR-ready with EU data residency, so sensitive customer data stays safe.
